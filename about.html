Why Choose BCA?

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program is an ideal choice for students passionate about technology and computer science. It provides a strong foundation in programming, software development, and database management, opening doors to various career opportunities in the IT industry. BCA equips you with industry-relevant skills in languages like C++, Java, and Python, preparing you for roles in software development, networking, and IT consulting. Additionally, BCA offers a pathway to further education, such as pursuing an MCA, making it a versatile and valuable degree for aspiring tech professionals.

Eligibility Criteria for BCA

To enroll in the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program, students must meet the following criteria:

  1. Educational Qualification: Must have completed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board or institution. A minimum aggregate percentage may be required (varies by college).

  2. Subject Requirements: Students from any stream (Science, Management, or Humanities) can apply. Having Mathematics or Computer Science as a subject in 10+2 is preferred but not mandatory in some colleges.

  3. Entrance Exam (if applicable): Some colleges may require candidates to pass an entrance examination.

The criteria may vary slightly depending on the college under Pokhara University. Always check individual college requirements for precise details.
