Introduction

Pokhara University is one of the leading institutions in Nepal, offering a wide range of academic programs. Among these, the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program stands out as a popular choice for students aspiring to build a career in information technology. This project aims to develop a static website using HTML that provides comprehensive information about the BCA colleges affiliated with Pokhara University. The website will serve as a resourceful platform for prospective students to explore college details, such as their addresses, principal names, contact information, available faculties, and facilities.


About BCA Course In Nepal

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a four-year undergraduate program designed for students who aspire to build a career in the field of Information Technology. It focuses on providing a strong foundation in computer science concepts and practical skills in programming, web development, and software engineering.


Objective

To create a simple yet effective HTML-based website that organizes and presents relevant details about BCA colleges under Pokhara University in a user-friendly manner.
